About Me

Ever since I was a little kid, I have always been fascinated by the world of coding and technology. From a young age, I would spend hours playing around with the command console on my computer, trying to understand how it worked and what it could do. I was constantly curious about the inner workings of computers, and I would often wonder about how these metal machines could create something from nothing.

As I grew older, my interest in technology only grew stronger. I began to experiment with different programming languages and technologies, and I even managed to create a mediocre mod for the game Cave Story when I was eleven. This mod added new dialogue and humorous text replacements, and it felt like a huge accomplishment for me at the time.

Today, I remain excited to continue exploring the world of technology and to use my skills to create innovative solutions for real-world problems. Whether it's building a new website, creating a custom software application, or developing a new mod for a favorite game, I am always eager to tackle new challenges and learn new things.
